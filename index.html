Classes

Beginning Knitting. No skills required, just enthusiasm. Make sure to come in early and purchase materials so class can start on me. Class size is limited. Learn the basics of knitting - cast on, bind off, knit, purl, increase and decrease, plus a few tricks. All materials included! Class is three 2-hour sessions. Fee: $55.00.

We stock over 125 different yarns in different colors and weights including handspun.

Products

We carry a wide variety of supplies for crochet, spinning, felting, and weaving as well as over 125 types of fine yarns. You'll find a great selection of natural fibers including wools, alpacas, cottons, linen as well as some lovely synthetics and other fiber arts.

We also carry many patterns including patterns from Cabin Fever, Knitting Pure and Simple, Fiber Trend, FiberSpace, Dovetail Designs and more!
